How to Make Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a common delicacy especially to the coastal towns in Kenya. In this article, I will be showing you the simplest way to prepare a very tasty chicken biryani. To start off, you must have the following ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or beef (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil rice until 70% cooked, then drain.
  3. In a pot, heat oil or ghee. Sauté onions until golden brown.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  5. Add yogurt, spices, and meat. Cook until meat is tender.
  6. Layer rice over meat. Sprinkle mint and coriander.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 15–20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with salad. Enjoy!

Tips

  • Use aged basmati rice for best texture.
  • Marinate meat with spices and yogurt beforehand for deeper flavor.
  • Don't overcook the rice, it finishes cooking during low heat.
